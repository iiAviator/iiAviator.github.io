About

My name is Mithun, I am an aspiring developer based in Dallas, Texas. Ever since I started programming when I was a child, I was always interested in making my life easier through different types of applications. This interest expanded with me through high school where I took various programming classes and I started going to popular contests both online and offline for programming. I started in Python, moved up to Javascript and then started learning Data Science using Python and Java. Here are a few projects of mine.

My skills include:
